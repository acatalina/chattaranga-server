A better way to learn a language...

Why so serious?

We're here to shake things up a bit and have some fun. So put down your textbooks and hold up on memorising those verb conjugations. There's a better way to learn. It's simple, it's natural. It's Chat-taranga. Jump into a collaborative learning environment and discover the way language is supposed to be learned. Have fun and let the chat flow.

Superior learning.

Chat-taranga connects you with other like-minded language learners and lets you discover a language the right way - by actually speaking it. By immersing yourself in the right environment and learning from each other, you'll be amazed at how much you can progress.

Join a community and grow together.

Become a member of the fastest-growing online language platform on the web and discover a global community, where everyone has something in common - the desire to learn. Chat-taranga is collaborative, fun and inclusive to all ability-levels. Whether you want to connect with a learner of similar ability, or find someone more advanced who can teach you more in 3 minutes that you could learn from 3 hours of classroom work, you can find it all here on Chat-taranga.
